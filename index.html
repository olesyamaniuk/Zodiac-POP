Game Description

Dice Masters: Snakes & Ladders brings the timeless board game to life with an epic upgrade. Face off against real players, choose your hero, unlock skills, and use powerful dice to climb the ladders and avoid the snakes. It's not just luck — it’s strategy, timing, and mastering the roll!

Features

Unique heroes with upgradeable skills

Classic Snakes & Ladders gameplay with a PvP twist

Real-time battles with players around the world

Strategy-based dice mechanics

How to Play

1
Choose your Dice Master and enter a match.
2
Roll the dice to move across the board.
3
Land on ladders to climb or snakes to fall — just like the classic rules.
4
Use hero abilities to gain an edge.
5
Outsmart your opponent and reach the top first to win!
